Featured Products and Solutions


Spectra Trading Company offers a diverse range of high-quality products tailored to meet the dynamic needs of various industries. From advanced automation systems to reliable power and energy solutions, Spectra ensures precision, durability, and innovation in every offering. With a focus on enhancing operational efficiency, the company provides cutting-edge products that cater to manufacturing, automotive, electronics, energy, and construction sectors. Below is a detailed breakdown of the featured products:

    A. Control Devices and Signaling Equipment

  • Proximity Switches: Inductive and capacitive sensors for precise detection in automated systems.

  • Limit Switches: Precision and oil-tight limit switches for industrial applications.

  • Control & Signaling Devices: Modular push buttons, rotary switches, and emergency stop buttons for control panels.

    B. Energy and Power

  • Circuit Breakers: Miniature circuit breakers (MCBs) with tripping characteristics for reliable power management.

  • Industrial Plugs and Sockets: Durable and weather-resistant plugs and sockets for secure power connections.

  • Energy Meters: Accurate power meters for monitoring energy consumption and optimizing usage.

    C. Cable Management Systems

  • Drag Chains: Cable carriers designed for high-speed operations and harsh environments.

  • Flexible Conduits: Polyamide and metallic conduits with IP68 protection for durable cable management.

  • Cable Glands: Polyamide and metallic glands for secure and sealed cable connections.

    D. Automation and Control Solutions

  • Panel Manufacturing: Customized control panels for seamless industrial operations.

  • Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs): Advanced PLCs for automated processes and system control.

  • HMI (Human Machine Interface): Intuitive interfaces for enhanced system monitoring and control.

    E. Specialized Products

  • PCB Design and Assembly: High-quality PCBs tailored to specific industrial requirements.

  • Temperature Controllers: Reliable controllers for precise temperature regulation.

  • Industrial Sensors: Photoelectric, fiber optic, and rotary encoders for diverse sensing needs
